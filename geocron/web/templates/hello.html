Location Aware

By combining your present location with the time of day, geocron automates your life. When you get to your Metro station during the commute home, geocron can send a text message reading "Pick me up dear" to your spouse.

Three Questions

Answer three simple questions to set up a geocron rule: Where am I? When am I there? What do I want to happen?

By harnessing your location data via Google Latitude, geocron can keep up with you all day. Just set some rules to trigger actions, and you're all set!

Right now, you can send emails, text messages, and hit webhooks with geocron.

Get Started

geocron requires a Google Account to log in, and it uses the Google Latitude and Google Places APIs.

You will be redirected to Google's account service to log in. You must enable Google Latitude with your mobile device.
